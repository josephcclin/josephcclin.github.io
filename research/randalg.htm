Randomized Algorithms


This webpage serves as resources for studying randomized algorithms. Some slides here are modified from the lectures of Professor S.-C. Tsai and Professor H.-I. Lu.

Textbooks
  • Randomized Algorithms. Motwani, R. and Raghavan, P., Cambridge University Press, 1995. [cover]
  • Probability and Computing: Randomized Algorithms and Probabilistic Analysis. M. Mitzenmacher and E. Upfal, Cambridge University Press, 2005. [cover]
  • The Probabilistic Method. 2nd Edition. N. Alon and J. H. Spencer, Wiley-Interscience Series in Discrete Mathematics and Optimization, 2000. [cover]
